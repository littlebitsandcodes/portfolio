Data + Design Multimedia Bio
Christina Li is a visual storyteller, drawing from documentary filmmaking, journalism, and design to craft visual and interactive stories. She is especially interested in examining media, culture, and social institutions.

Currently working on projects related to: transformation of documentary storytelling in the streaming era, female friendships on TV, horror movies’ fixation on women, and cop cities and the language of policing.

Data Viz • Mapping • Scrollytelling
